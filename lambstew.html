Lamb Stew

Description

lamb is the meat of kings, and theres no better way to consume it than with a flagon of mead, and a piece of hard bread

Ingredients

  • lamb (duh)
  • broth (not vegatable)
  • a cauldron

Steps

  1. get a fire going for your cauldron
  2. chop up the vegatables
  3. put the stuff in the pot
  4. make the stew
